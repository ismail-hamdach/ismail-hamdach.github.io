Fintech Campus Ecole Nationale des Sciences Appliquées de Berrechid

FINTECH CLUB aims for establishing fintech culture within our compus ENSA BERRECHID and introduce students to the technologies that serve this domain ( blockchain, cryptocurrency, cybersecurity..). We as students can participate in the development of the fintech field in Morocco through our visions as engineering students with the support of experts.

Who We Are

Fintech Club ENSAB

FINTECH ENSA BERRECHID directed by Morrocco FINTECH Association was created in 2021 by some student of ENSA BERRECHID motivated by the new technologies that aims to improve the use of financial services thanks to those members and for their hard working , several individuals deserve special mention : SALMA BOUSTANI, IKMRAM EL MALKI, SALMANE MOUADDAB, ISMAIL HAMDACH, CHAIMAA ROUIJEL, BADER AL QARAOUI and YOUNES EL HAIMER
